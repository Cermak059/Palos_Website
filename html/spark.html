RIVAL RUMBLE
Compete in a "best frame" competition where everyone has a chance to win one for the team. The player with the best score in each frame counts towards the composite score for the team. Teams choose from creative mascots like the Unbeatabulls, Lil' Dragons, KillerBees and more! At the end of the game each player earns their own rewards on both teams. Rival Rumble is the ultimate team game!

BOOM BOTS
is a 5 frame, 1 bowl game. Developed exclusively for Spark, Boom Bots turns the lane into an interactive scoring experience. Pins and targets on the lane are worth big points, but the targets are worth more. Take aim at this fast paced and fun demolition filled game.

PIN PIX
Lets guests take photos, use images from their mobile devices or take pictures from the lane tablet. 10 frames of fun using filters and photo editing tools just like your favorite apps. Only pin pix lets players photobomb each other while collecting hats, beards, glasses and other wacky accessories!

ANGRY BIRDS
is a 5 frame game where the pigs are identified by the bowling pins in random order per frame on the overheard or on the lane itself when spark is active. The object...Get all the pigs! You have 2 shots per frame to pop all the pigs, if you get them all on the first shot without a strike, the machine will reset for next player. Watch for special bird levels that pop pigs easier! Max score is 250,000.
